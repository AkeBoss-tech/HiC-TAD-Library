How to read the triangle TAD plot

  • x-axis: genomic position (bp)
  • y-axis: genomic distance / 2 — the farther from the x-axis, the more distant the two interacting loci are
  • Dark upward triangles = TADs (self-interacting domains)
  • Valleys between triangles = TAD boundaries / insulators
  • Cyan dashed lines = insulator deletion boundaries
  • In difference panels: red = gained contacts, blue = lost contacts after deletion
